Attire

We love being Seattle’s dressy restaurant. We recommend a suit or sport coat for men, and ask that no casual attire or shorts be worn. On this night, you can’t be overdressed, so go big and have fun with it.

Vaccines

We strongly encourage our guests to be vaccinated but will not require verification to enter our dining room at this time. Our service staff wear masks and local governance requires guests to wear masks while not eating. We will continue to update this policy as state and local mandates evolve.

Children

All ages are welcome in our dining room, but we ask that everyone be prepared for a lengthy dining experience. We don’t allow tablets or phones to entertain kids as this can be a distraction for other guests.
